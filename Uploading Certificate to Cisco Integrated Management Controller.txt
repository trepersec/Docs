Uploading Certificate to Cisco Integrated Management Controller (CIMC)

1. Login
2. Admin > Security Management (default is Certificate Management)
    - To access the Navigation pane, select the icon in the top-right corner of the web UI
3. Generate CSR and fill in accordingly
4. The CSR will be downloaded > Copy the CSR into your CA > Download the Certificate (BASE64) generated by the CA
5. Go back to Certificate Management > Select Upload Server Certificate Upload > Selecting Upload will immediately reset CIMC (~30s)
