Downloading/Uploading Apache OpenOffice (Ubuntu)- https://www.openoffice.org/download/

(1) tar Apache-OpenOfficeX.X.X.tar.gz

(2) cd ~/Downloads/en-US/DEBS

(3) sudo dpkg -i *.deb
By default, this will install/update Apache OpenOffice in your /opt directory.

(4) cd ~/Downloads/en-US/DEBS/desktop-integration

(5) sudo dpkg -i *.deb
